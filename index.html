Our Idealogy.

Arewa Innovate is a technology-based start-up company with the primary aim of bridging the gap in knowledge and application of computer and other tech-related skills in Nigeria with special emphases in the north, through sensitization & Awareness, service provision, Training and Mentoring among others. The company is driven by the desire to provide tech solutions to the ever-growing challenges, especially in Northern Nigeria. We are confident this can be achieved through innovative and tech-based research and development approaches. We are committed to providing opportunities and jobs to many young people thereby harnessing the huge human resources in Nigeria
